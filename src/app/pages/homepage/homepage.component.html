Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

To rent a car, you need a valid driver's license, credit card, and must be at least 21 years old. For specific car models, additional requirements may apply. Please contact our customer service for detailed information.

The minimum rental period for self-drive cars is 24 hours. However, we offer flexible rental packages to accommodate your needs, including hourly options for certain vehicles.

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance to ensure vehicle availability, especially during peak seasons. Last-minute bookings are possible but subject to availability.

Airport pickup and drop-off services are complementary for rentals over 3 days. For shorter rentals, a nominal fee applies depending on the distance.

In case of an accident, contact our 24/7 emergency support immediately. All our vehicles come with insurance coverage, and our team will guide you through the necessary procedures.
